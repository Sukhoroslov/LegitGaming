Welcome to the World of Outlast

Step into the nightmare that is Mount Massive Asylum. Uncover the dark secrets hidden behind its walls, survive its twisted inhabitants, and escape the horrors that await. Outlast challenges players not only with its terrifying atmosphere but also its ability to immerse them in a deeply unsettling psychological thriller, where every decision and movement can determine life or death.

The Horror Awaits

Outlast is a survival horror game that challenges you to survive in a dark and terrifying environment. As investigative journalist Miles Upshur, you must explore Mount Massive Asylum, a facility overtaken by terrifying creatures and the twisted experiments of the Murkoff Corporation. The game’s design makes use of realistic physics, detailed sound design, and a dynamic lighting system to create an atmosphere of dread, where every corner holds potential for both discovery and danger.

With nothing but a night vision camera and your wits, you’ll navigate the dark hallways of the asylum, avoiding deadly enemies and uncovering the shocking truth behind the madness that consumes the asylum. Every moment spent in the game heightens the tension as you begin to understand the horrors that have been unleashed in this forgotten place.

Uncover the Secrets

Every corner of Mount Massive hides a new horror. The walls speak, the shadows move, and nothing is as it seems. Will you uncover the truth or become another lost soul within the asylum’s dark embrace? As you progress through the game, you’ll encounter a range of characters, some seeking salvation, others serving as agents of the malevolent forces that govern the asylum. These characters serve as essential components of the narrative, and their tragic fates serve as a testament to the twisted experiments carried out by Murkoff.

Explore the gameplay, characters, and lore to better understand the terrifying world of Outlast. Each piece of the story you uncover reveals more about the asylums' sinister history and the experiments that led to the creation of the horrifying entities that now roam its halls. The truth is hidden behind layers of deception and only the most determined will uncover it all.
