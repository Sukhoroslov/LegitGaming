What is a Platform Game?

Platform games are one of the most enduring genres in video games. Players control characters who run, jump, and climb through environments, avoiding traps and enemies.

  • Super Mario – The most iconic platformer series, featuring power-ups and classic levels.
  • Donkey Kong – One of the earliest platformers, introducing climbing and barrel-dodging mechanics.
  • Sonic the Hedgehog – A game-changer in platforming with speed-focused gameplay.

Popular Sonic Games

  • Sonic the Hedgehog (1991) – The game that started it all, introducing loops and speed-based gameplay.
  • Sonic CD (1993) – A time-travel adventure featuring animated cutscenes.
  • Sonic Adventure (1998) – The first 3D Sonic game, setting the stage for modern platformers.
  • Sonic Mania (2017) – A critically acclaimed return to 2D platforming.
  • Sonic Frontiers (2022) – A fresh take on open-world exploration and high-speed action.
