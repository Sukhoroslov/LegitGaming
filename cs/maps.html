CS:GO 2 Maps

Dust II

One of the most iconic maps in Counter-Strike history, Dust II is set in a Middle Eastern environment. It is a balanced map offering exciting gameplay for both attackers and defenders.

With its simple yet effective layout, Dust II is beloved for its ability to provide endless strategic opportunities and unforgettable matchups.

Whether you're holding the bombsite or pushing into enemy territory, Dust II promises a thrilling and intense experience.

Inferno

Set in a quaint European village, Inferno challenges players with its narrow corridors and strategic chokepoints. Teamwork and precise tactics are essential to dominate this map.

Inferno is renowned for its intense firefights and the importance of utility usage to control key areas like Banana and A Site.

To succeed, players must master the map's angles and tight corners while communicating effectively with their team.

Mirage

A desert-themed map with a mix of open spaces and tight corners. Mirage is a favorite among professional players due to its strategic depth and versatility.

This map rewards players who can think on their feet and adapt to the dynamic flow of the game.

Mirage features one of the most balanced layouts in the game, where both teams can execute complex strategies or engage in high-speed firefights.

Nuke

Nuke takes players to a high-stakes industrial facility. With its multi-level design and vertical gameplay, it offers a unique challenge for strategic minds.

Mastering the transitions between the upper and lower bombsites is crucial to success on this map.

The intense vertical gameplay on Nuke demands precise coordination and quick decision-making under pressure.

Overpass

A map that combines urban and industrial elements, Overpass rewards strategic planning and team coordination. Its dynamic environment keeps players engaged throughout.

Overpass stands out for its emphasis on map control and the ability to outmaneuver opponents with clever plays.

Take control of key areas like A Long and B Connector to dominate the map and secure victory.
